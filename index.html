How to use with NotebookLM

  • 1
    Browse the categories in the sidebar and select an industry that matches your presentation topic.
  • 2
    Explore the tailored slide blueprints and click the Copy Prompt button for the layout you like.
  • 3
    Paste the prompt directly into Google's NotebookLM alongside your source documents to generate highly customized and beautifully structured slide content!
  • ※
    Pro Tip: All prompts are highly customizable. Feel free to swap out the suggested colors, fonts, and visual references within the prompt text to match your specific brand identity before generating!

Combine Style & Content

Note that our prompts focus exclusively on presentation design and structure. To get the best results, we highly recommend combining these design blueprints with your own content prompts.

Pro Tip: First insert the copied design prompt, then add your own content instructions (e.g., "Write a 10-slide deck summarizing our Q3 Marketing Strategy from the attached PDF"). This creates a powerful all-in-one instruction for NotebookLM, Gemini, or Claude!
